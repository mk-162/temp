## Market Positioning
- **High Volume/High Efficiency:** Uline
- **Technical/Spec-Heavy Buyer:** ZipTie.com (Navigation wins here)
- **Specialized/Custom Needs:** Cable Ties Unlimited (Custom printing, exotic materials)
- **Price Sensitive/Small-Medium Biz:** Cable Ties & More
- **Specification Driven:** Panduit / HellermannTyton
